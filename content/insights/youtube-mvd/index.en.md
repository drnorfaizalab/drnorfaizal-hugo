## Key takeaways

> Early diagnosis makes a significant difference in outcomes. If you or a family member are experiencing persistent headaches, unexplained vision changes, or new neurological symptoms, please seek evaluation promptly.

### When should I be concerned?

The symptoms below warrant prompt neurological assessment:

- Headaches that are new, worsening, or present first thing in the morning
- Seizures with no prior history of epilepsy
- Weakness, numbness, or speech difficulty that comes on suddenly
- Personality or cognitive changes noticed by family members
