## Brain. Head. Pain.

In the operating theater, every millimeter is the boundary between hope and loss. This is the world that shaped my soul and discipline during my four years at the University of Oxford.

I am often asked: ***Why does a neurosurgeon who treats complex tumors also focus on pain management?*** The answer is simple: **Because quality of life is a fundamental human right.** Untreated pain is a prison, and I am here to unlock the door.

### Three Pillars of Expertise:

1. **The Brain:** ***Uncompromising Precision*** | At the peak of my expertise is the anatomy of the brain. Treating tumors and neuro-oncology cases is more than a technical procedure; **it is a trust** to preserve the memory, personality, and functions that make us human. Here, technological brilliance meets absolute Discipline.

2. **The Head:** ***Restoring Life*** | Chronic headaches and migraines are often underestimated, yet they are ***"thieves of time."*** I use a neurosurgical lens to look behind the curtain of your pain—**seeking definitive solutions for neuralgia and migraines that have failed to respond to standard medications.**

3. **Pain:** ***Freedom from Suffering*** | Prolonged pain should not be your destiny. Through minimally invasive interventional techniques, I apply the precision of a brain surgeon to treat spinal and nerve pain. My goal: **to stop pain at its source so you can return to living with dignity.**
